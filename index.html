About us

Jewels Crush is an exciting puzzle game where players match sparkling jewels and gems to solve captivating puzzles and unlock magical rewards. With smooth gameplay, stunning visuals, and progressively challenging levels, this game offers endless fun and entertainment for puzzle lovers of all ages. Enjoy a refreshing and addicting experience while you collect jewels and crush through dazzling challenges.

FAQ

  • How do I start the game?

    Download the app, install it, and press 'Play' to begin your jewel-matching adventure.

  • Is the game free?

    Yes, Jewels Crush is free to download, with optional in-app purchases.

  • Can I play offline?

    Yes, Jewels Crush supports offline play, so you can enjoy the game without an internet connection.

  • What are boosters?

    Boosters are special items that help you complete puzzles more efficiently, clear difficult levels, and earn extra rewards.

  • How do I unlock new levels?

    Complete the current levels to unlock new, more challenging puzzles and progress further in the game.

  • Can I play with friends?

    Yes, you can connect with your friends, share your progress, and compete on leaderboards to see who can achieve the best scores.
